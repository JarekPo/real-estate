About Us

Our Company

We are a trusted estate agent

Real Estate Agency was founded by Igor and Jaroslaw in 2021. We are at the forefront of modern real estate brokerage in Ireland. Property management is our passion and we are proud to provide services for this purpose. We are a well known local company that operates in the Irish market. We operate professionally and reliably and always put the customer first, whether you are a landlord, a tenant or just looking for professional advice. So if you own a property and would like to sell or rent it, you have come to the right place. Our agency will guarantee it. Give us a call, visit our office or send us a message. Together we are sure to meet your property management needs.

What We Do

We will help you to sell, buy or rent any resitental property

We provide professional services in the sale of real estate for residential purposes. We assist, advise and mediate in the sale of all types of residential properties. We operate locally but also deal with properties throughout Ireland. We will help you if you own a property and want to sell it. If you dream about your own house, we will help you to achieve this goal. If you own a property and would like to rent it out, we will rent it to you in no time. If you want to rent a cosy flat, we will help you to find it. We guarantee to bring out the full potential of your property in the sale and rental process - we make the transaction the most advantageous. We conduct our services in a pleasant, fun and friendly atmosphere and strive to establish a friendship with our clients that will last for years.

We are also planning to expand our offer with commercial properties.
